EduKathon Program

CECAF Association, in partnership with the Aoual Association, announces the launch of the second edition of the “EduKathon” program for the benefit of male and female students the city of Mohammedia, aged between 15-18 years, in order to encourage them to follow the principles of social innovation and enhance their life skills. Participants will benefit from training workshops, orientation sessions, and local and national competitions on social innovation in order to present their project ideas.

Within the framework of the EDUKATHON program organized by Cecaf Association and the Anoual Association, in partnership with the Regional Directorate of the city of Mohammedia, a communication meeting was organized on Sunday, February 11, 2024, at the Joulan School, in the presence of 98 male and female students accompanied by a group of administrative and pedagogical frameworks from various schools in the city.

Within the framework of the EDUKATHON program organized by Cecaf Association and the Anoual Association in partnership with the Regional Directorate of the city of Mohammedia, a training meeting was organized on Sunday, February 18, 2024, at the Golan High School, in the presence of 90 male and female students accompanied by a group of administrative and pedagogical frameworks from various qualifying high schools in the city.
